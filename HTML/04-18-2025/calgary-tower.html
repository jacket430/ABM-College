Calgary Tower

Rising 191 meters above downtown Calgary, the Calgary Tower is an iconic landmark that offers breathtaking 360-degree views of the city and surrounding Rocky Mountains. Since its completion in 1968, it has been a symbol of Calgary's growth and development.

Key Features

The Calgary Tower offers unique experiences and attractions:

Observation Deck

Experience panoramic views from the glass floor observation deck, offering a unique perspective of the city below and the majestic Rocky Mountains in the distance.

Sky 360 Restaurant

Dine in style at the revolving restaurant, enjoying fine cuisine while taking in the ever-changing views of Calgary's skyline.

Historical Significance

Learn about the tower's construction during Calgary's oil boom and its role in the city's development as a major Canadian metropolis.

Special Events

Host your special occasion in one of the tower's unique event spaces, perfect for weddings, corporate events, and celebrations.

Visitor Experience

  • Glass floor observation deck
  • Interactive exhibits about Calgary's history
  • Fine dining at Sky 360 Restaurant
  • Special event hosting
  • Gift shop with unique souvenirs

Visitor Information

Location

101 9th Avenue SW, Calgary, AB

Located in the heart of downtown Calgary, easily accessible by public transit and the CTrain.

Hours of Operation

  • Observation Deck: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM daily
  • Sky 360 Restaurant: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM daily
  • Extended hours during special events
  • Last elevator ride 30 minutes before closing

Accessibility

The Calgary Tower is fully accessible and includes:

  • Wheelchair-accessible entrance and elevators
  • Accessible washrooms
  • Assistive listening devices available
  • Accessible parking nearby
  • Service animals welcome

Plan Your Visit

To make the most of your visit to the Calgary Tower, we recommend:

  • Visiting during clear weather for the best views
  • Reserving a table at Sky 360 for a special dining experience
  • Checking the sunset times for spectacular evening views
  • Allowing 1-2 hours for a complete visit
  • Exploring the surrounding downtown area
