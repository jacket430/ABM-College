Welcome to Calgary

Calgary, the largest city in Alberta, is where modern urban living meets western heritage. Known for its stunning skyline against the backdrop of the Rocky Mountains, Calgary offers a unique blend of contemporary city life and rich cultural history.

As you explore our website, you'll discover the diverse attractions that make Calgary a must-visit destination. From the iconic Calgary Tower to the historic Heritage Park, each site tells a unique story of Alberta's past and present.

Why Visit Calgary?

  • World-famous Calgary Stampede
  • Close to the Rocky Mountains
  • Rich Indigenous and Western heritage
  • Vibrant arts and culture scene
  • Several popular conventions and events every year

Whether you're planning your first visit or returning to explore more of Calgary's treasures, our website will help you discover the best of what this dynamic city has to offer.
