Studio Bell

Experience the heart of Canadian music at Studio Bell, home of the National Music Centre. This stunning architectural landmark celebrates Canada's musical heritage through interactive exhibits, live performances, and an impressive collection of musical instruments and artifacts.

Key Features

Studio Bell offers unique experiences and attractions:

Interactive Exhibits

Engage with hands-on exhibits that explore the science of sound, music production, and Canada's rich musical history through innovative technology and displays.

Historic Instruments

View the world's largest collection of Canadian-made pianos and rare instruments, including the Rolling Stones Mobile Studio and Elton John's piano.

Performance Spaces

Experience live music in state-of-the-art venues, including the 300-seat performance hall and intimate recording studios that host concerts and events year-round.

Architecture

Admire the striking design of the building, featuring nine interlocking towers that create a unique silhouette on Calgary's skyline and symbolize the connection between music and community.

Visitor Experience

  • Interactive music-making stations
  • Guided tours of the collection
  • Live performances and concerts
  • Educational programs and workshops
  • Special exhibitions and events

Visitor Information

Location

850 4th Street SE, Calgary, AB

Located in the East Village neighborhood, adjacent to the Calgary Central Library and easily accessible by public transit.

Hours of Operation

  • Wednesday to Sunday: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Closed Monday and Tuesday
  • Extended hours during special events
  • Last admission 30 minutes before closing

Accessibility

Studio Bell is fully accessible and includes:

  • Wheelchair-accessible entrance and elevators
  • Accessible washrooms
  • Assistive listening devices available
  • Accessible parking nearby
  • Service animals welcome

Plan Your Visit

To make the most of your visit to Studio Bell, we recommend:

  • Checking the calendar for special events and performances
  • Allowing 2-3 hours for a complete visit
  • Participating in interactive exhibits
  • Taking a guided tour for deeper insights
  • Exploring the surrounding East Village neighborhood
