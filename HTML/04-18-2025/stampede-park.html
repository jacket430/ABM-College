Stampede Park

Experience the heart of Calgary's western heritage at Stampede Park, home to the world-famous Calgary Stampede. This iconic venue combines thrilling entertainment, agricultural excellence, and western hospitality year-round.

Main Attractions

Stampede Park offers a variety of attractions and experiences:

Stampede Grounds

Explore the historic grounds that host the annual Calgary Stampede, featuring rodeo events, chuckwagon races, and agricultural exhibitions.

BMO Centre

Visit Calgary's premier convention center, hosting major events, trade shows, and exhibitions throughout the year.

Saddledome

Experience world-class entertainment at the iconic Scotiabank Saddledome, home to the Calgary Flames and major concerts.

Elbow River Camp

Discover the rich Indigenous culture at the Elbow River Camp, featuring traditional tipis and cultural demonstrations.

Year-Round Activities

  • Agricultural shows and competitions
  • Conventions and trade shows
  • Sports events and concerts
  • Cultural festivals and celebrations
  • Educational programs and workshops

Visitor Information

Location

1410 Olympic Way SE, Calgary, AB

Located just east of downtown Calgary, easily accessible by public transit and the CTrain.

Operating Hours

  • Grounds: Open daily, hours vary by event
  • Calgary Stampede: 10 days in July
  • BMO Centre: Varies by event
  • Saddledome: Varies by event

Accessibility

Stampede Park is fully accessible and includes:

  • Wheelchair-accessible facilities
  • Accessible parking and drop-off zones
  • Assistive listening devices
  • Accessible washrooms throughout the park
  • Service animal accommodations

Plan Your Visit

To make the most of your visit to Stampede Park, we recommend:

  • Checking the event calendar for current activities
  • Planning ahead for major events and purchasing tickets early
  • Exploring the grounds during non-event days for a quieter experience
  • Visiting during the Calgary Stampede for the full western experience
  • Taking advantage of public transportation to avoid parking challenges
