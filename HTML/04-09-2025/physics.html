"Physics"

Course Description

Welcome to "Physics" class, where we explore the fascinating world of doing absolutely nothing! This course is perfect for those who want to master the art of avoiding responsibilities while maintaining the illusion of productivity. Our lab is equipped with comfy chairs, blankets, and an impressive collection of excuses.

Course Objectives

  • Master the physics of falling asleep in class (terminal velocity of eyelids)
  • Calculate the optimal velocity for running away from responsibilities
  • Develop advanced napping techniques (including the "pretend to take notes" position)
  • Study the aerodynamics of paper airplanes (for throwing at the teacher)
  • Write a thesis on "The Relationship Between Snoring Volume and Grade Point Average"
