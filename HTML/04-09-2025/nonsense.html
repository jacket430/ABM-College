Nonsense

Course Description

Welcome to Nonsense, where we delve into the intricate study of meaninglessness and its profound implications. This course explores the theoretical underpinnings of nonsense as both a linguistic construct and a philosophical concept. Through rigorous analysis, students will examine how nonsense challenges our fundamental assumptions about communication, logic, and reality itself.

Course Objectives

  • Investigate the semiotic structures that define and deconstruct meaning
  • Analyze the role of nonsense in linguistic evolution and cognitive development
  • Examine the philosophical implications of meaninglessness in contemporary thought
  • Study the intersection of nonsense and creativity in artistic expression
  • Write a thesis on "The Ontology of Nonsense: A Post-Structuralist Approach"
