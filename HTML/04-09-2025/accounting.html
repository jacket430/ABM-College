"Accounting"

Course Description

Welcome to "Accounting," where we explore the fascinating world of creative bookkeeping and number interpretation. This course examines the theoretical foundations of accounting practices that defy all mathematical logic, from the art of making numbers disappear to the science of creative financial reporting. Through rigorous practice and analysis, students will master the subtle nuances of making the books balance through sheer force of will.

Course Objectives

  • Master the fundamental principles of creative bookkeeping
  • Develop advanced techniques in number interpretation
  • Analyze the psychological impact of imaginary financial statements
  • Study the historical evolution of accounting magic
  • Write a thesis on "The Art of Making Numbers Disappear: A Theoretical Analysis"
