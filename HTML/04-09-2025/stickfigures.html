Advanced Stick Figures

Course Description

Welcome to Advanced Stick Figures, where we explore the theoretical foundations of Stickpage.com and its impact on digital culture. This course examines the complex art of stick figure animation and game design, from the philosophical implications of stick figure violence to the psychological impact of flash-based entertainment. Through rigorous analysis and hands-on practice, students will master the subtle nuances of creating stick figure masterpieces that may or may not involve excessive violence.

Course Objectives

  • Master the fundamental principles of stick figure animation
  • Develop advanced techniques in stick figure combat choreography
  • Analyze the psychological impact of stick figure violence
  • Study the historical evolution of Stickpage.com and its cultural significance
  • Write a thesis on "The Semiotics of Stick Figure Violence: A Post-Modern Analysis"
