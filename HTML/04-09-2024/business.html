"Business"

Course Description

Welcome to "Business," where we explore the complex art of paper airplane management and corporate silliness. This course examines the theoretical foundations of business practices that make absolutely no sense, from the strategic deployment of paper airplanes to the management of imaginary meetings. Through rigorous practice and analysis, students will master the subtle nuances of pretending to be busy while doing absolutely nothing.

Course Objectives

  • Master the fundamental principles of paper airplane aerodynamics
  • Develop advanced techniques in imaginary meeting management
  • Analyze the psychological impact of pretend deadlines
  • Study the historical evolution of corporate nonsense
  • Write a thesis on "The Art of Looking Busy: A Post-Modern Analysis"
