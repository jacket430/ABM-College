"Biology"

Course Description

Welcome to "Biology", where we explore the fascinating world of plant consciousness and their secret lives. This course examines the theoretical framework of plant sentience, their complex social structures, and their hidden agendas. Through rigorous observation and analysis, students will uncover the truth about what plants really think about us.

Course Objectives

  • Investigate the cognitive capabilities of various plant species
  • Analyze the social hierarchy within plant communities
  • Examine the psychological impact of photosynthesis on plant behavior
  • Study the complex communication networks between plants
  • Write a thesis on "The Secret Lives of Houseplants: A Psychological Study"
