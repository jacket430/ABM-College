Absurdity

Course Description

Welcome to Absurdity, where we explore the fundamental contradictions of existence and the inherent meaninglessness of human endeavors. This course examines the philosophical foundations of the absurd, from ancient paradoxes to modern existential dilemmas. Through rigorous analysis, students will confront the inherent contradictions of reality and the human condition.

Course Objectives

  • Analyze the ontological implications of absurdity in human existence
  • Examine the role of paradox in shaping human consciousness
  • Investigate the relationship between absurdity and existential anxiety
  • Study the manifestation of the absurd in contemporary society
  • Write a thesis on "The Dialectics of Absurdity: A Post-Modern Perspective"
