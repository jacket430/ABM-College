Our 'Academic' Programs

At Silly Goose College, we believe in the power of silliness to transform lives. Our rigorous academic programs are designed to challenge conventional thinking and explore the boundaries of traditional education.

Arts Department

  • Silliness 101 - The foundation of all our programs
  • Nonsense - Advanced studies in meaninglessness
  • Absurdity - The art of being completely ridiculous
  • Kid Pix - Digital art at its finest
  • Finger Painting - Advanced techniques in condiment-based art
  • Advanced Stick Figures - The complex art of minimalist human representation

Science Department

  • "Physics" - The theoretical study of doing absolutely nothing
  • "Chemistry" - Advanced studies in "special" cookie making
  • "Biology" - The study of plant consciousness and their secret lives

Commerce Department

  • "Economics" - The study of imaginary numbers and pretend money
  • "Business" - Advanced techniques in paper airplane management
  • "Accounting" - Creative bookkeeping and number interpretation
