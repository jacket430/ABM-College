Kid Pix Studies

Course Description

Welcome to Kid Pix Studies, where we study the most revolutionary art program of the 90s! Since our computers are broken, we'll be doing this course entirely through the power of imagination and Wikipedia articles. Students will learn about the rich history of Kid Pix, its impact on digital art, and what it would look like if our computers actually worked.

Course Objectives

  • Memorize the Wikipedia article about Kid Pix
  • Draw Kid Pix-style art on paper with crayons (we ran out of markers)
  • Pretend to use the "Wacky Brush" tool
  • Make "computer noises" with your mouth while drawing
  • Write a 10-page essay about what Kid Pix would look like if our computers worked
